 * *Label* = atlanto-occipital joint
 * *Definition* = The Atlanto-occipital joint (articulation between the atlas and the occipital bone) consists of a pair of condyloid joints. The atlanto-occipital joint is a synovial joint. The ligaments connecting the bones are: Two Articular capsules; Posterior atlantošccipital membrane; Anterior atlantošccipital membrane; Lateral atlantošccipital
