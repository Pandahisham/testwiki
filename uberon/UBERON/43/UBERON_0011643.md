 * *Label* = puboischiofemoralis internus muscle
 * *Definition* = a single large dorsal limb rotator muscle in lower tetrapods that runs from the lumber region and girdle to the femur.
 * *Comment* = Taxon notes: 3 muscles differentiate from this: psoas, iliacus and pectineus.
