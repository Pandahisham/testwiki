 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0009568
 * *Label* = trunk region of vertebral column
 * *Definition* = Subdivision of vertebral column that corresponds to the trunk of the body, containing the trunk/presacral vertebrae. In organisms that have a thoracic and lumbar distinction, this corresponds to the sum of both of these regions.
