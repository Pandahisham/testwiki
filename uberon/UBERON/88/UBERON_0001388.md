 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001388
 * *Label* = gastrocnemius
 * *Definition* = A muscle of the shank. In mammals it has two heads[Kardong]. It runs from its two heads just above the knee to the heel, and is involved in standing, walking, running and jumping. Along with the soleus muscle it forms the calf muscle. [WP].
