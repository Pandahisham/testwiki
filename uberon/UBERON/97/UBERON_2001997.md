 * *Label* = parietal-supraoccipital
 * *Definition* = Compound dermal and endochondral bone that results from the fusion of the paired dermal parietal bones and the median endodermal supraoccipital. Forms the dorso-posterior element of the dermatocranium.
 * *Comment* = A parietal-supraoccipital or parieto-supraoccipital bone is an element present in Siluriformes.
