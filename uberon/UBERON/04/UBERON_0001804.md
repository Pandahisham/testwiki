 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001804
 * *Label* = capsule of lens
 * *Definition* = a smooth, transparent basement membrane that completely surrounds the lens. It is synthesized by the lens epithelium and its main components are Type IV collagen and sulfated glycosaminoglycans (GAGs).

## Synonyms
