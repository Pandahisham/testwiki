
## Class : posttemporal fossa

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_2001753
 * *Label* = posttemporal fossa
 * *Definition* = Fossa where the exoccipital forms the medial portion and the epioccipital forms the lateral margin.
 * *Comment* = Chen 1994 gives these two synonyms: "temporal fossa", Gauba 1967; and "temporal groove", Mahajan 1966.
