 * *Label* = popliteal area
 * *Definition* = The popliteal fossa is a space or shallow depression located at the back of the knee-joint. The bones of the popliteal fossa are the femur and the tibia. Colloquially, it is referred to as a 'knee pit.'
 * *Comment* = a depression on the ventral side of the femur, between the condyles, presumably related in some fashion to the popliteal nerve and/or artery.[Palaeos]
