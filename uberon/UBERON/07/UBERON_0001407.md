 * *Label* = semispinalis thoracis
 * *Definition* = The Semispinalis dorsi (or semispinalis thoracis) consists of thin, narrow, fleshy fasciculi, interposed between tendons of considerable length. It arises by a series of small tendons from the transverse processes of the sixth to the tenth thoracic vertebr¾, and is inserted, by tendons, into the spinous processes of the upper four thoracic and lower two cervical vertebrae.
