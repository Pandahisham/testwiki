 * *Label* = conjunctival fornix
 * *Definition* = line of reflexion of the conjunctiva from the eyelid to the eyeball.
 * *Comment* = Forms the junction between the bulbar and palpebral conjunctivas. It is loose and flexible, allowing the free movement of the lids and eyeball
