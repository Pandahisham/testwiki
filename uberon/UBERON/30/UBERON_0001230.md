 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001230
 * *Label* = glomerular capsule
 * *Definition* = The Bowman's capsule is a cup-like sac at the beginning of the tubular component of a nephron in the mammalian kidney. A glomerulus is enclosed in the sac. Fluids from blood in the glomerulus are collected in the Bowman's capsule and further processed along the nephron to form urine. This process is known as ultrafiltration. [WP,unvetted].
 * *Comment* = One fold of the S-shaped body gives rise to Bowman’s capsule[GUDMAP]
