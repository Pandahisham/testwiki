
## Class : obsolete CoPA

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_2005175
 * *Label* = obsolete CoPA
 * *Definition* = An interneuron in the spinal cord. CoPA are bipolar with "T" shaped dorsal dendrites, have contralateral longitudinal axons that crosses the cord dorsal to the Mauthner neuron and ascends in the dorsal cord.
 * *Comment* = This term entered TAO as a leaf node in the zebrafish AO and is not in the CL. incorrectly generalized to teleosts
