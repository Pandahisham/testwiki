 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0006651
 * *Label* = appendix testis
 * *Definition* = The appendix testis is a vestigial remnant of the Mullerian duct, present on the upper pole of the testis and attached to the tunica vaginalis. It is present about 90% of the time.
 * *Comment* = Editor notes: the label hydatid of morgagni is ambiguous w.r.t male vs female. The ncita structure 'hydatic of morgani[sic]' is male and thus belongs here. See also 'vesicular appendage of epoophoron'
