 * *Label* = gill opening
 * *Definition* = Anatomical space that is the external opening of a gill slit connecting the pharyngeal cavity to the exterior. Gill opening is usually paired and located on the lateral or ventral surfaces of the head.
 * *Comment* = In teleosts, there is typically a single gill opening posterior to the operculum.
