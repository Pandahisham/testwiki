 * *Label* = calcaneofibular ligament
 * *Definition* = A skeletal ligament that connects the fibula to the calcaneus. In humans, it is a narrow, rounded cord, running from the apex of the fibular malleolus downward and slightly backward to a tubercle on the lateral surface of the calcaneus. It is covered by the tendons of the Peron¾i longus and brevis.[WP,modified]
