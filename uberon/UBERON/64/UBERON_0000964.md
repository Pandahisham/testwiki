 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0000964
 * *Label* = cornea
 * *Definition* = transparent front part of the eye that covers the iris, pupil, and anterior chamber. Together with the lens, the cornea refracts light, accounting for approximately two-thirds of the eye's total optical power.
 * *Comment* = Taxon notes: Compared to terrestial animals, the cornea [of zebrafish] is relatively flat. It consists of nonpigmented, stratified squamous nonkeratinizing epithelial cells, attached to a thick basement membrane that is considered to be analogous to the Bowman’s membrane in mammals

## Synonyms

 * cornea of camera-type eye
 * tunica cornea
