 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0005333
 * *Label* = mammary bud
 * *Definition* = Mammary gland buds form by an outpocketing of the mammary placodes and grow to invade the mammary fat, when they form the mammary cord[GO]. produced along the length of the embryonic mammary ridge these buds represent the future locations of the mammary glands. Invagination of ectoderm at each bud leads to the development of epithelial diverticula, later maturing as lactiferous ducts[TFD].
